The governor-general thought it politic to send Dhuleep Singh,
the young king, with some ceremonial to his palace, he accordingly issued the following general
order, which made a favourable impression on the inhabitants of Lahore, as well as on the chiefs
of the Sikh nation:--

"The right honourable the governor-general requests that the commander-in-chief will cause
the following arrangements to be made for escorting his highness the Maharajah Dhuleep Singh
to his palace, in the citadel of Lahore, this afternoon.
