These mixtures, created with various herbs gathered by shepherds, were sold publicly in Rome. Remoras, also known as sucking-fish, specific frog bones,  starfish, and hippomanes are also used to create the solution. Horace informs us that dried human marrow and liver were could also be used in the concoction. "That his parch'd marrow might compose... together with his liver dried... (creates) an amorous dose."
