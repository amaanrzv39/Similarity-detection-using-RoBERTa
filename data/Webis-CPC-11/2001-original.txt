Accustomed to work in a groove
himself, his mind never travelling beyond the downs on which his life was spent, he could not
fathom the divine purpose in placing her under his care, but yet being quite clear in his own
mind it was God's will for her at present, he did his duty towards her without questioning;
but the idea of Jack rising out of his own sphere of life into a higher was another matter
altogether.

"I don't know," said John, at last, as Fairy repeated her question.
