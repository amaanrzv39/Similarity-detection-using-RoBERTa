The work is dedicated to
Pius II. in honor of his recent elevation to the papal throne, and since this is evidently
the dedication copy, the accession of Enea Silvio Piccolomini in August, 1458, fixes approximately
the date of the MS. In April, 1460, Jacopo Zeno was translated to the see of Padua.
