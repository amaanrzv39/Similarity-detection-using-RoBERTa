He took up two of them and compared the answers given to the same question
by the two men.  The answer required was a translation of a passage of Greek with notes. And,
as far as I can remember, his words were these: "Now, W----, this man has passed over the real
difficulty.  As far as I can tell, he has not even noticed that there is a difficulty.  I have
given him two marks out of a possible ten.  This other man has seen the difficulty and grappled
with it.
