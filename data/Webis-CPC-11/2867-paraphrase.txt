In the words of Prof. Henry is the sound of whistle is distributed horizontally. It is much stronger in plane contains the lower edge of the bell rather than other side. If the whistle standing in ordinary position, its sound is more distinct in a horizontal plane through the whistle than above or below it.

The steam fog-whistle is same instrument which used in steamboats and locomotives. It is from 6-8 inches diameter and is operated by the steam of pressure from 50-100 pounds.  An engine is  used to same boiler and an automatic arrangement is used to openit and closing its valves at determined times.
