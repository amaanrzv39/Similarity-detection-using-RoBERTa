They sustained their march the next dawn. The effects of the rays of the sun reflected from the snow winning the eyes, produces a disease, which the Peruvians call strumpet. It occasions blindness, accompany by excruciating pains. A pimple forms in the eye-ball, and causes an itching, pricking pain, as though needles were continually piercing it. The temporary loss of sight is occasioned by the impossibility of opening the eye-lids for a single moment, the smallest ray of light being totally insupportable...
