Rowdy pigs dropped the passers-by off the sidewalk; tipsy pigs hiccoughed their variety of "We won't depart household till morning," from the gutter; and very fragile youthful pigs tripped daintily through the mire, as if they plumed themselves upon their ankles, and kept themselves mostly organised in purpose of stockings. Maternal pigs, with their fascinating families, strolled by in the sun; and often the pink, baby-like squealers lay down for a nap, with a hope in Providence worthy of human imitation.--Hospital Sketches.

SELECTIONS FROM TRANSCENDENTAL WILD OATS.

On the first day of June, 184-, a large wagon, drawn by a tiny horse, and comprising a motley consignment, went lumbering over certain New England mountains, with the gratifying accompaniments of airstream, precipitation, and hail.
