"No potatoes?  is the sky falling?" She asked.  "You have been eating mashed potatoes with cream and a little beetroot with meat for lunch for years."

"No, we can't eat potatoes anymore.  Didn't you hear?"

"I heard something about it, but can't we eat those we already have?"

"No.  We have to give them away.  Remember?  Just cold mutton and salad.  No toast.  No toast anymore."
