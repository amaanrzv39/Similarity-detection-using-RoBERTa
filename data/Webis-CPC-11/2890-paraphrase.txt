He only had  little funds with him.  He had barely enough amount to  run his business upto the time till he could have a legal auction. But still there is the problem of rent. At last the landlord agreed to wait for  his rent for a very high rate of interest.The pawnbroker again became cheerful. His daughter advised him to pay the rent and cease his business for the time being. But he did not agree with her.  When the crisis was end he distributed his money to the people who brought him  their old clothes and worthless jwellery even though it is worthy for them
