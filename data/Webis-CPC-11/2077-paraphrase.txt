But towards destroye everybody without consyderacyon, is and wyll be unto Englande for ever, a most horryble infamy amonge the  solemn senyours of else nacyons. A great nombre of them whych purchased these superstycyouse mansyons booked of those lybrary bokes, a number of towards serve theyr jakes, a number of towards scoure theyr candlestycks, and a number of towards rubbe theyr bootes. Some they sold towards the grossers and sopesellers, and a number of they posted again see towards the bokebynders, not within low nombre, but at tymes complete shyppes full, towards the wonderynge of the foren nacyons.
