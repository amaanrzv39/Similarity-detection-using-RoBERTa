I continued, 'Only,' playing my part with as much skill as I had, and up until now more than I could have believed myself capable of just a few days ago, 'I don't want to get imprisoned."

'No, no, I don't want you to get locked up either, Jacky, I should miss you, you know, very much.' he answered, 'But you act sensibly, and you will be all right.  What's more, I will show you how to make your fortune before we have done.'

With perfect truth I stated: "I should like to make a fortune."
