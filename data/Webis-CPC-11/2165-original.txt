*"Shaw Empty of Good Sense"*

By Christabel Pankhurst.

Written for THE NEW YORK TIMES.

His reputation for perversity and contrariety is fully maintained by George Bernard Shaw in
the ineptly-named article, "Common Sense About the War." At home in Britain we all know that
it is Mr. Shaw's habit to oppose where he might be expected to support, and vice versa.
