Annual reviews of Schiller literature appear within the Jahresberichte fuer neuere deutsche Litteraturgeschichte and  within the Berichte des Freien Deutschen Hochstiftes. Valuable  specially for its English titles is the bibliography compiled via John P. Anderson for Nevinson's Life of Schiller, London, 1889.

EDITIONS

During the lifetime of Schiller his writings were printed within different forms via different publishers, and owing towards the absence of copyright unauthorized reprints were numerous.
