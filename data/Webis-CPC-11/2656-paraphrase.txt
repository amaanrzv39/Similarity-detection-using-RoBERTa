The Minute Book on January 12th, 1673 assured that the members “consented that Mr. Riveley and Mr. Morley should attend upon the court to carry their Order for appointing the time for the Ministers Meeting at the Library for future to be upon the first Tuesday in every month.”  They were grated the request.  The Court ordered ‘36s to be paid for six Russia leather chairs for the City Library,” on 29th March, 1673. {8}

There is not a regular entry of receipts issued for fees and charges, but there are occasional records of receipts and payments throughout the Minute Book.  there was an entry under March 3, 1684, as follows: “The account of your last year was stated, this day.  4ll 3s & 4d and had expended 4l 11s 10d--due to Him 8s 6d." were received by the Library keeper.
