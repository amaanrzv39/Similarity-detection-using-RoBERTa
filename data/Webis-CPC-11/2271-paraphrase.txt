Letters were wrote, from dates November, through December 1744, between Lord Granville, and the duke of Somerest, about titles on the death of the Duke's Grand son.

There were autographed notes from 1762 to 1763, from Gearge III, adressed to Charles, Earl of Egremont, on the topic of public business.  

In a letter dated January 1st 1761, to the Earl of Egremont, enclosed was complimentary verses to the Lady Egremont, written from Lord Lyttleton.
