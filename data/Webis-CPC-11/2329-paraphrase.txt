The loud blast filled the air and was heard by many. Miss Marigold cried, "I declare, it's that filthy fellow Punch coming afore our vindow vith his imperence; I prognosticated how it voud be, ven the alderman patronised him last veek by throwing avay a whole shilling upon his fooleries." The alderman relied to Miss Marigold, "You've no taste for fun, Biddy." While speaking to Miss Marigold, the alderman placed a hand on me and my daughter's shoulder.
