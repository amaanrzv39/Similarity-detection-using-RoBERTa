And yet this estimable lady, fresh from the drawing-rooms of Upper-Radcliffe-on-the-Hudson
and the ballroom of Rector's, thought how "delightful" this would be! Ah, fortunate indeed
is it that the pluck and persistence of the pioneers carved a way of peace for the pilgrims
of today!

Considering the foregoing, such a book as this, presenting as it does in readable form the
Arizona West as it really was, is, in my opinion, most opportune and fills a real need.
