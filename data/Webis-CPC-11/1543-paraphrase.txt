The word "thou" as used in English is fearful all the time; but it natural like a leopard in the forest.  The beast says, "But thou shalt write in English."

"Spare me!" I dared to say.

"In English," the best continued, relentlessly--"in wild, insane, incorrect English.  And the story shall be of France--France, where women always depart from the dinner table before the men.  Keep this in mind, and utilize it at the ninetieth page of your first volume.
