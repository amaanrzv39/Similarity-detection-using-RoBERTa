Three asterisks before a title indicate the best and least permanent literary value of the story and entitles it to a place on the yearly "Rolls of Honor." An asterisk before the name of an author indicates that he is not an American. Cross references after an author's name refer to previous volumes of this series.(H) after author's name indicates that other author's stories published between 1900-1914, are indexed in "The Standard Index of Short Stories," by Francis J. Hannigan, published by Small, Maynard & Company, 1918. The figures in () after the story's title refer to the volume and page number of the magazine.
