He was devoted to his profession so much that he began to lose interest in other aspects of his life; forgoing meals and sleep, and being indifferent toward his dressing. Had it not been for his filial daughter, he would have been a very sorry state. Indeed, he was extremely fond of her, even more so than his clocks.  

However, not all thought well of him; some even believing him to be mentally unsound. His neighbours, in particular, belittled his art and mocked his character and lifestyle.
