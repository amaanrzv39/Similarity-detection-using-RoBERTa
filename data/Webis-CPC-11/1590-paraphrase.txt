These, built on the same plan as ours are, but with flat bottoms, belonged to traders, & were solely adapted to the transport of merchandise. The stern, fancifully ornamented, rises four or four stages above the deck, & is the stool of the helmsman. The inside of the boat is filled with goods, & thatched over, leaving sufficient room underneath to accommodate four or four families--men, women, & children--who promiscuously take up their abode there.
