The King must have been satisfied, for besides a payment of 5000 marks, I received in a couple of days through Wedel a diamond pin and a wonderful gold watch and chain with the incription of the Grand Ducal arms of Mecklenburg-Schwerein  

"For services rendered faithfully to my house." 

Chapter VI. The Intrigue at Monte Carlo 

Meahwhile, in Berlin from a mission to Vienna, my letters delivered, once more comfortably residing in my quarters, on the Mittelstrasse, I was anticipating an evening at the Pavilion Mascotte.
