Although young, he was twentyseven , Lucan produced several shorter poems, an epic he finished 10 books that relates wars of Caesar and Pompey, and contains many fine and striking images and thoughts. He prefer Pompey to Caesar and processed a strong love of liberty. His languages is pure and his poem often harmonious,  he never attains the singular delicacy and sweenness of his master, Virgil.
C. Silicus Italicus was lived during in the region of Nero, his birth place was unknown. He was the Consul in the year of A.D.68. He was a stoic, he put and end on his life in the year A.D.100, when he was 75 years of age.
