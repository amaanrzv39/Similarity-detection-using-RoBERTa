"If Vidocq should see them he would mistrust some plot, and then farewell
to the bird."--"But where shall I place them?"--"Oh, why in this closet--mind, no noise, that
would spoil all; and I have more desire than yourself that he should not suspect anything."
My commissary was now shut up in four walls with his agents. The door, which was very strong,
closed with a double lock. Then, certain of time for escape, I cried to my prisoners, "You
are looking for Vidocq--well, it is he who has caged you; farewell."
