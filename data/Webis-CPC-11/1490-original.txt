
In vain Dumps declared his ignorance of the virtues of "medicinal gums." The more he protested,
the more the old woman sued; when to his horror a reinforcement joined her from the cottage,
and men, women, and children implored him to cure the good dame's malady. At length watching
a favourable opportunity, he insinuated his heel into the side of the piebald, and trotted
off, while entreaties mingled with words of anger were borne to him on the wind.
