The Duke of Palma having secretly married La Felina in the town of Ferentino, the day Monte-Leone
recognized him, took his beautiful wife to a villa he possessed on the lago di Como, and after
sojourning there a few days, went to Naples and forced the King to accept his resignation as
minister of police. The Duke was dissatisfied with Naples, for no one would forgive him for
marrying the Prima-Donna.
