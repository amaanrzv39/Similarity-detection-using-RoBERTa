"If you approve, now after having a pleasure meeting with you, I will make sure you reach home quite safely" he said in reply to her query "Why this honor?". This was answered only by way of a smile as nothing else could match it.

After throwing himself away from the horse, he turned towards his house with the bridle in his hand, with a look at the girl as if asking her to accompany him. Leam could not, but had to accept the offer his escort, as a matter of course. She was thinking why? She felt very pleasant to have the company of a person, who is not his fater - as she was very shy with him. She was always reading the past in the eyes of his master, who was very kind and tender, but she was held in his hand as a master could.
