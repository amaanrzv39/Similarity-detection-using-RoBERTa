She said, 'Were you sound asleep, very highly charge papa?' 'Oh no!' he answered; 'only I have such pleasant thoughts.' During his disease his hands were often collapsed in prayer; and when he did not chat, his serene face presented that the 'sweet thoughts' were with him to the end."

'On the after twelve noon of Saturday, the 14th of December, it was noticeable that the end was near. "Gutes Frauchen" ("Good little wife") were his last doting remarks to the Queen as he kissed her and then taking it easy his head upon her shoulder. A little while later the Queen crooked over him and said, "Es ist kleins Frauchen" ("It is little wife"); the prince obviously knew her, though he could not chat, and bowed his head in response.
