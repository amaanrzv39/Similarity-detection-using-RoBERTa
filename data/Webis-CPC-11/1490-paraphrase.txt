Dumps declaration of his ignorance of the goodness of “medicinal gums” did not have any effect.  When he griped hard, the old woman sued much; a support joined her from the cottage to his alarm, and men, women, and children beseeched him that the good dame’s illness be cured.  He insinuated his heel into the side of the piebald, and hurried of, when he got the right time to escape.  Even as he fled, he could hear the plea mingled with words of anger follow him on the wind.
