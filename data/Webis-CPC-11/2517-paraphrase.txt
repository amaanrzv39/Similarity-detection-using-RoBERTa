Several Asiatic species trees like Siberian pine, larch and cedar  also grow in north-east and certain shrubs and herbs of Asiatic steppes spread in to the south-east.  But all these does not affect the general pattern of vegetation. 
          The region of steppes can be divided into two parts namely the intermediat zone and the proper steppes.In the former both climates exists ie west-European and Asiatic. So the vegetation also differ.  As the climate vary the vegetation also vary from forest to Steppe.
