"And you'd be right to do so, Sir," said Ramee.  "There is nothing quite like a good dinner when I'm hungry!  And when I remember that Amphitryon is Henry the Fourth's grandson, my pleasure is doubled at least!"

The duke replied, "My dear, Ramee, no one gives a compliment quite like you!"

"But I do not compliment," Ramee cut in.  "I only say exactly what I think."
