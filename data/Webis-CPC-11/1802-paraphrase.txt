She said to him in a kind manner: "Shall I read to you?" He replied in bitterness: "Don't want reading." But she went on: "Shall I write to any of your friends?" But he even more bitterly answered: "I hav'n't any friends." In a kind manner, she took an orange from a basked and offered it to him. His face was brightened, but he just answered, in that usual bitter voice: "Don't care if I do."
