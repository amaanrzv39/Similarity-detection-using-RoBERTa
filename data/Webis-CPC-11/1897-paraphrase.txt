Gazing at the fascinating beauty around me, I feel light as air, fresh like morning, and blissful such as martyr at the death gate.

A voice sighed in low and mellifluous like the wind-harp that parley with the sweet south breath, "This is a ravishing and radiant spot with its bowery beauty that make you stop and admire the Golden City splendour, the Fairies City! Thrice mortal joy! thither, even to our city, Come! i am assign to conduct you."
