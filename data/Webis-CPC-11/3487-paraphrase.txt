A defeat of all defeats had been suffered in the most painful and humiliating way. Domestic feeling had been not less severely wounded by professional feelings. A kinsman had just been struck dead, another bleeding from wounds. Courage was sustained by a firm faith in God, and a high sense of duty to the state. In the midst of misery and disgrace, still hold your head nobly erect and found fortitude, not only for you but for those around you. Be sure of your road. The gentle manner removed their apprehension: their language had been familiar in childhood; and he retained enough of it to communicate with them.
