They originated from a Mole Hill and the fine music had always been accompanying them. As was commly seen in england  they used to  dance in  moonlight days or form in the shape of a Ring. In many of the districts  they dance at a place where mushrooms grow and be there as a foreground for the dancers.This suits very well for the little people  who were accorded with the stature.The Mole hill also works as a forground. But the tree,Satanic head in the foliage does not make good match.
