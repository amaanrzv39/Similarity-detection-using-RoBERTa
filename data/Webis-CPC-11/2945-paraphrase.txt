The core of this society is formed by Fontenelle, Montesquieu, Marian, Helvestius, the hearer, Marivaux and Astruc. Marmontel, informs us what is the nature of the society was and speaks of their hunting after lively pride and brilliants flashed of wit, disapprovingly.  He is however not suitable to the society, because it was necessary to have real knowledge and a deeper train of thought than he was capable of.
