" It's getting late, and I have to go now," she said.
"Tomorrow?" I asked in anticipation.
"Yes, tomorrow, I'll see you soon, Goodbye" she said as she turned to leave.  She turned back as if she had changed her mind, but then continued on her way, only to dash my hopes.  If the tables were turned, I would not leave her in this compromised state.

3. Evening, and a Castle Under Attack

There is a thick forest surrounding Tiverton Manor, where I went to be alone, lying in the bed of leaves, daydreaming of happier times.  Somehow, I could feel the crumpled papers in my jacket pocket.
