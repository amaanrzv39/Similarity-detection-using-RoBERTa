"If Vidocq should see them he would distrust some plot, and then farewell to the bird."--"But where shall I place them?"--"Oh, why in this closet--mind, no noise, that would spoil all; and I have more wish than you that he should not think anything." My commissary was now shut up in four walls with his agents. The door, which was very well-built, closed with a double lock. Then, sure of time for escape, I cried to my prisoners, "You are looking for Vidocq--well, it is he who has caged you; farewell."
