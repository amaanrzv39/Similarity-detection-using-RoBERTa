Wear the white silk outfit that was to have been my wedding garb, including the veil and the silk orange blossoms.  You'll find them in the third drawer, and you'll find my keys.  Aunt Pen was muttering this and said, "He'll get it should he arrive...the Bride of Death."

Then she was silent and so were we.  Mel was sorry and restless.  We hadn't known that Aunt Pen had had a true love.
