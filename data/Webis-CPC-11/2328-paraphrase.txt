They were on in years, and their bodies unbending and they were close to destitute, was there anything to be grateful for? For arthritis? Yes, if that's how He would have it, according to Aunt Betsey, though she was nowhere close to grateful.

After opening the door, they were brought to attention by the piquant aroma of food being prepared.

"Hello, Uncle Jake, what you bin doin'? what's you messin' wit yer?"

Uncle Jake dropped his jaw, as if he couldn't get enough of the smells.

"Is dis dose chillen? Who's bin cookin'? Gracious! Is dis a king festin's?"
