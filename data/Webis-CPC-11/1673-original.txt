On another occasion, the people
to whom he had preached entreated their minister to try and get him again, and if he could
not preach to them, that at least he should pray once more with them.

He was not, however, long absent from home on this occasion. On the 25th I find him recording:
"Reached home; entirely unprepared for the evening. Spoke on Psalm 51:12, 13, 'Restore unto
me the joy,' etc. There seemed much of the presence of God,--first one crying out in extreme
agony, then another.
