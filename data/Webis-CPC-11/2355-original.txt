
Whereupon these two delightful imps chuckled and wagged their heads with a sincere enjoyment
that this mere world could not give! Johnny slipped off his shoes and stockings and hurriedly
put them on the infant Florry, securing them from falling off with a thick cord. This added
to their enjoyment.

"We can play cubby house in the stone heap," whispered Florry.

"Hol' on till I tote in this wood," said Johnny. "You hide till I come back."

Johnny swiftly delivered his load with an alacrity he had never shown before.
