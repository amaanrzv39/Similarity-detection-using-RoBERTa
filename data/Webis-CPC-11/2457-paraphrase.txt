He would surely be a captive for a hundred years should he decide to pass through the holes. The fragments in the holes would then dash out and snatch him up the tree from which they originated and capture him.

Quicoy was so delighted by the thought that he could capture the Ongloc, and decided to go for it that very night. Some of the boys that he asked to accompany him said that he must go by himself or else the charm would have no effect. But they warned him to be cautious enough not to pass the line between the hollows or suffer the consequence of being a captive just like the Ongloc.
