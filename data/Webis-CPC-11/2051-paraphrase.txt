One was asked whether he did not wake with the fall from the bed. He replied the other that he felt nothing till he awoke. He was in the shoe receptacle as if he was in his tomb. They  told him that Europeans never tumble out of their beds. He replied that he has lost all hopes of transforming into an European.
    And decides to sleep on the ground as in the past, from which there is no danger of tumbling. 
    The hippopotamus, or river-horse, is found exclusively in the great rivers, lakes, and swamps of Africa.
