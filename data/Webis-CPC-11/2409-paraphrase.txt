Brembre was mayor in 1377 and in 1383 - 85.  In London he led the group of King Richard's friends.  His public career is covered in other places.  But with regard to his financial transactions, the abstracts following contain necessary informtion.  In September 1377, the king borowed L10,000 from Brembre, Wallworth, Philipot, and John Haddele (who was a grocery merchant and after that the Mayor of London), among others, and pledged the crown jewels.
