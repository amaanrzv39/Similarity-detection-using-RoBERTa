Surely, Earth's governments had created their own terrible weapons.

We watched, breathless.


The pencils with green light no longer posed a danger to our world which now appeared to be far away. The great ring with the empty space in its rim wabbled with uncertainty for a few seconds as though it was upset from within and being torn apart. Then it went directly for the Pioneer. We were watched.

But Hart was up for the moment and he shot the Pioneer toward the earth at such speed that we all were flattened to our supports with the exact same violence. Then we had an equally violent deceleration.
