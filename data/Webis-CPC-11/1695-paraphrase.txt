On the one hand there is, rapine, plunder, slavery, tribute, civil disability; on the other hand, security, peace, and honor. We need not be surprised that, under such restraint, conquered peoples succumbed before Islam. Nor were the chronological inducements to change confined to the period during which the Saracens were engage in spreading Islam by force of arms. Let us come down a couple of centuries from the time of Mohammed, and take the reign of the tolerant and liberal-minded sovereign, Al Mamun.
