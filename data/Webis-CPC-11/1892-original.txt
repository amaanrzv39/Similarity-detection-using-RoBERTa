Violent
drastics are wrong, they do not do good; you cannot go on giving physic every day, this will
teaze the bowels and not tranquilize them, The cure is to repeat the excitement of progressive
action. People in general will not find out that what may be an adequate excitement one day,
may not be an adequate excitement on another day. As to these things, they are easily managed,
and you should attend to them. Every person advanced in life knows this, and attends to it.
