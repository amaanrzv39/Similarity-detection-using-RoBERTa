
The unblushing parade of forms of vice, which have been manufactured in the Orient especially
to meet the demands of renegade members of Christian civilization, can be seen in a peculiarly
painful and brazen form in the city of Hong Kong.

While we were at Hong Kong, there occured a great celebration in honor of the repair and rededication
of an important Buddhist temple. There was a grand procession, and many thousands of Chinese
from the mainland came over to witness the celebration.
