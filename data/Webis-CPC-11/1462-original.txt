The air, too, has been examined as well as the water, and, so far as yet
ascertained, the proportion of iodine in the atmosphere is variable, and much greater in amount
in some regions than in others. The activity prevailing in this particular branch of inquiry
is the more encouraging, as the maladies which it aims at removing are of so peculiarly distressing
a nature; and the investigation is one likely to lead also to valuable incidental results.
