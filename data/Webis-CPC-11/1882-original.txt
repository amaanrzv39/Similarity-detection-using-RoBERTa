And if it won, perhaps--perhaps--he might not scorn her! At
any rate it was a sacrifice, and sacrifice for him was an attractive thought to her.

"Me ruin him?" she said to Barbara. "Don't you be too sure! There is a shorter and a better
way nor yours, to save him, an' I'm goin' to try it!"

The bluegrass girl, astonished, would have questioned her, but Madge waited for no questioning.
Without another word she hurried from the room, in a mad search for Colonel Doolittle.
