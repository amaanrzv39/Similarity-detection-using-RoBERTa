The explanation given is that during his past crisis no on offered any aid from man nor supernatural power.  Sadly he states, "We must tread the wine-press alone."  His saving grace appears to his sense of self sustaining power by using his creative talent that had been bestowed upon him.  Of this awareness, he could not be robbed and this is the driving force of the fragment and not Titanism of the Prometheus of Aeschylus.
