Within these limits there is no one but has claims to bring
forward; the discussion is open to all the world, the public will pronounce its definitive

As a general thesis, I will add that by concentrating our researches on one circumscribed and
special object, we have a better chance of seeing it correctly and knowing it well, all other
things being equal, than by scattering our attention in all directions.

As to the merit of contemporaneous narratives, it seems to me very dubious.
