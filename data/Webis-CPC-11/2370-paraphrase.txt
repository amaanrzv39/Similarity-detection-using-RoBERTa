"He inquired my name and laughed when I told him.  After I showed him where we lived and explained that you might give him some money, he also laughed and I ran away, frightened because all the other girls had also run."

At this story, her father's face changed from mischievous to worried and he listened carefully.

"You're a foolish girl," he answered, after a pause,"but you shouldn't speak to men you don't know, especially those who ask questions."
