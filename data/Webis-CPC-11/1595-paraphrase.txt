Last came Osito's mother and placed a small yet hansom brown bear skin on the mound. It was tastefully bejeweled with blue ribbons. In the center of the tanned side there was a drawing, in red dye, the outlines of a very stolid and stoical-looking pappoose. 

Outside the small village of Katrine, where the country turns into town, a circus put up its tents. On the greens the canvas pyramids were fastened.
