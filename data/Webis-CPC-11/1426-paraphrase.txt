Well, recall I am ready to lend you a hand, and pull you through when things get desperate." The smirk with which this offer was received had no effect on my companion. They took it as a tribute to the subtle humour which, as they believed, lay lurking in his simplest utterances. "Always make 'em laugh," they observed, with pride. "It keeps up the spirits of these poor devils of rowing-men; and elderly BULLEN knows I am all there when I am wanted."
