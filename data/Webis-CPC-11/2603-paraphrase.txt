1. This is not such a ruddy light appears in the eclipses, When we may discerne the obscurer parts of the moon Why can we not see the like rednesse?

Perhaps, you may say, the light takes away that appearance then the neerenesse of that greater light.

About my reply, this cannot be, When he is neere the Moone? then why does Mars shine with his wonted rednesse. why cannot her greater brightness make him appear whit as other Planets? Any reason given why that greater light should represent her body.
