. S. DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE 
FARMERS' BULLETIN No. 1408 

The HOUSE FLY AND HOW TO SUPPRESS IT 

Keeping files effect cleanliness and sanitary conditions. Without adequate disposal of substances also harmful. They are very dangerous, it will badly affect health and lead to desease.

We should know how to breed and where to breed. It is important the application of such knowledge in combating them. In addition to giving direction to ridding the house of files, this bulletin gives advise to the use of screens, fly papers, poisons and flytraps. It looks on the methods of eliminating breeding places snd preventing the breeding files.
