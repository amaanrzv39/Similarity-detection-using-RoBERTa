132. Then the Priest (or Bishop if he be present) shall let them depart with this Blessing.

For the meaning of the expression 'or bishop if he be present,' see the note on rubric No.
110, p. 35.

The words 'let them depart' imply that the congregation are not to dismiss themselves previously.
