Poor Ellen! what should I do without her!"

"Fairman, join me in wishing success to our young friend here. Mr Stukely, here's your good
health; and success and happiness attend you. You'll find little society here; but it is of
the right sort, I can tell you. You must make yourself at home." The minister became more cheerful,
and an hour passed in pleasant conversation. At ten o'clock, the horse of Doctor Mayhew was
brought to the gate, and the gentleman departed in great good-humour.
