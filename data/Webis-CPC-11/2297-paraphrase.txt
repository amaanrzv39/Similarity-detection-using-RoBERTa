Soc.I would recommend not so more than to purchase lads and train them up from boyhood as farm-labourers. There are some certain happy moment from my opinion which must be seized, alike in man and horse which is rich in present service and in future promise. I can prove you that how men treat their wedded wives in such a way that they find true helpmates to the joint increase of their estate, while others treat them in a wholesale disaster to bring them upon themselves.
