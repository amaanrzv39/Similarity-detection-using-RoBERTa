Prepare solution with 1 part of soluble citrate iron, 1 part of red Prussia of potash and with 10 part of water. To make this solution use a brush like the brush for letter-press. Cover the surface of the paper with the solution. Then remove the prepared paper and pour clean water on it. Then hang it up in a clear sky for 20 to 30 minutes. The sensitive paper will be prepared very rapidly due to its ability to stand washing.
