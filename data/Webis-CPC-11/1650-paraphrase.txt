As the boat entered the inlet, they could see that the beach was full of rocks.  Inside the inlet, it became drastically quieter.

Hrolfur ordered aloud, "Loosen the foresail!"  Eric leaped up and held onto the sail, while Hrolfur loosened the mainsail, without letting go of the other sheet.  Probably, Hrolfur imagined with would be safer to sail into the gusty inlet with loose sails.

"Are we going to enter?" inquired the man who had just awakened from his slumber.
