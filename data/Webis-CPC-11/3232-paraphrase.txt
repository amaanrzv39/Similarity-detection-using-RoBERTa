Cross enough do you think, "Oh yes you can be."  Cross enough to eat you in a mouthful and also be a little sissy.  How funny it sounded.  Lottie laughed and so did I.  I was very nervous.  Then all at once Miss Grant grew very comicaly grave, and asked us whether we thought we should soon make her cross.  And then followed such a funny talk, "I think I should never forget this."  

Miss Grant was half laying on the sofa and Lottie and I was bobbing up and down beside her.  Sometimes looking right into her blue laughing eyes, and she was sometimes hidng her rosy face.  She didn't see how queer she made us feel.

You don't like the idea much of having a governess.  I said, "You fancy it will be a lesson, lessons all day long now, a great deal of crying and punishments, very hard things to learn and no fun any more."  If that's what it really is going to be, then I should get so unhappy that I should run away from home again.  And then you think I shall have to grow cross and ill-tempered, too-that is the worst part of it all.

She pretended to be ready to cry, so Lottie who didn't like to give up her own opinion muttered something about "She thought they always were!"
