Interest free loans are given for the books which are given in the mental hospitals and prisons which are located in the country and urban districts.
While the books, periodicals and other informations available to the country people are given in the following ways:-

(a) Free loans of books to libraries controlled by the local authorities so that they do not charge any thing for using the library and maintain the reasonable standard of library.

(b) Loans with a very little charge to independent subscription public libraries, charged on each set of 50 books.

(c) Loans of books to isolated group of readers at a small annual charge through hamper collections.

(d) Free loans of books for lighthouse keepers and very remote readers.
