
Our hearts grew heavier and heavier as we talked together. The Matron, said: "Why, I thought
when I came here it was to do a regular Christian work for these girls. That was my purpose,
but the more I inquire into the matter, and study over the things I am expected to do and ask
no questions, such as sending girls over to the Lock Hospital at the Chief Inspector's request,
the more I feel that I am being worked for purposes of which I cannot approve. I cannot stay here."
