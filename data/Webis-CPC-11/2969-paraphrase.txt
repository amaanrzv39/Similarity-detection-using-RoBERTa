He was the one who introduced best between wisdom extract by the sense of humour, and that obtained through the reason; and thus he associated the doctrine of innate ideas. From the uncertainty of wisdom derived through the senses, he concluded the twofold system of true and apparent knowledge. [Footnote: Prof. Brandis's article in Smith's Dictionary.]

   
   [Sidenote:Zeno introduces a new method.] 

Zeno of Elea, the friend and student of Parmenides, born B.C. 500, brought nothing new to the system, but invented Dialectics, that logic which later became too esteem in the hands of Plato and Aristotle, and so basically admired among the schoolmen. It needs to establish truth by removing fault by the reductio ad absurdum.
