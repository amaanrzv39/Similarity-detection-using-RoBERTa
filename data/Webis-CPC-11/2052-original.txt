
* * *

"Item, all the religious who are admitted and enter the monastery and religion, shall bring
one alb and one amice, to be delivered into the hands of the treasurer and preserved by him
for the use of the church.

* * *

"The treasurer is to have the books that are in daily use in the choir re-bound, and to see
that the capes which are unsewn, and all the ecclesiastical vestments under his care are kept
in proper repair.  He is to have the custody of the plate belonging to the monastery, and to
hold a key of the treasury.
