She was really beautiful, she was delicate, and looked like an aerial shape dancing in the peam of poes, she was gentle and playful and very expressive. She looked like a silvan goddes, which artists love to picture and whose employment is to chase one another on the green bank, or in the transparent wave.

Guillaume really loved the beautiful bourgeoise. Later he learnt that such love was a condescension. Amable inquired whether she possessed love at all when her father refused her heart to Guillaume. She then realized that she had given her heart to him a long time ago.
