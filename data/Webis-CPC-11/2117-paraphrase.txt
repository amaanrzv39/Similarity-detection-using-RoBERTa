Hereby appeares the obvious error of compelled plaines, viz. your crust in the smaller components, is couered with the crust of the higher components, and both with poorer earth: your heights hauing the crust taken away, are become meerely barren: in order that either you should force a new crust, or haue an euill soile. And be certain you leuell, before you vegetation, lest you be compelled to remoue, or injure your plants by cutting into, and casting amidst their roots. Your ground should be cleered as much as you may of pebbles, and grauell, partitions, hedges, bushes, & other weeds
