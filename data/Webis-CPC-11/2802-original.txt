Ladies' Fashions for the Spring.

The advance of the spring appears to have brought increase of gayety in London and in Paris,
in which cities fashionable society has received new impulses from circumstances connected
with affairs. Heavy velvets have generally given place to silks and satins, and there is a
prevailing airiness in the manner in which they are made up.
