
It fares not otherwise with the soule then with the body: besides the native & radicall heat,
the principall instrument of life, there are aguish and distempered heats, the causes of sicknesse
and death.

To discerne of those, requires some skill and judgement: yet a good Empirick, a Christian of
experience will give a shrewd ghesse at them, the easier & the better if he marke these following
signes and symptomes, common to all the kinds of false zeale, here also following.
