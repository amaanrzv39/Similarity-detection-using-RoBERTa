What a jest of fate!... Farewell forever, Luna!... No, not Luna. Farewell, Horabuena!

On the next day he took passage on the ship that was leaving for Port Said. What was there
for him to do in Gibraltar?... It had been for three months a paradise, at the side of the
woman who beautified his existence; now it was an intolerable city, cramped and monotonous;
a deserted castle; a damp, dark prison. He telegraphed to his uncle, informing him of his departure.
