She became serious in a moment when she saw I was hurt, and did not lose her good-temper at
my rude speech, but said pleasantly, "You are not fond of being teased, Mr. Highrank. Never
mind: I don't intend to take advantage of your blunder, nor keep you long in suspense. Go "--and
she smiled as if she really could not help it--"go, and be sensible in future."

"You mean that you won't marry me?" I asked.
