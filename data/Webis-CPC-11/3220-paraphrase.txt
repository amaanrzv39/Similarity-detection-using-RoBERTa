To secure a disciplined mind one have to practice from his boyhood itself. He must stick to certain principles like self-denial, obedience to the orders of superiors, respect and affection to the elders  etc.
       The officers of the education department have totake some measures to enhance this.
     The officers who are incharge of education system  must seen to it that the elementary education system should be uniform and form part of the curriculum. The teachers  should be qualified enough to give the necessary instructions.
