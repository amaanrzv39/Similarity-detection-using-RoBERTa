
"But did you not wake with the fall?"

"No; I felt nothing till I awoke, as I believed, in my tomb, but really in the shoe receptacle;
and since you all assure me that Europeans never tumble out of their beds, I resign all hopes
of ever being transformed into one. I shall in the future, as I have done in the past, sleep
on the ground, from which there is no danger of tumbling."

THE HIPPOPOTAMUS.

The hippopotamus, or river-horse, is found exclusively in the great rivers, lakes, and swamps
of Africa.
