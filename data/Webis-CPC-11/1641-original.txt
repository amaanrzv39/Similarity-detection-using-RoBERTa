Hanged the poor
devils were, and after that very much was made of Grifone. Amilcare took him through all his
campaigns, had him well taught, gave confidence for confidence, and found by the time he was
at Nona, making his "Gran Tradimento" of Farnese, that he could not get on without him. The
accepted remedy for such a state of the case was to kill the youth at once. Amilcare did not
do that, and at first was able to bless himself for his second forbearance.
