The letter purpose is to inform Don Tomas of his returning to England after claiming "magnificent lands," and to prepare his old acquintances to befriend him there. The Letter he wrote was answered by Castro, written by his son Pedro, He give him nomerous good wishes and news about Melipilla, and the old circle present condition. His Attorney, Mr. Holmes, is astonished and dismay as Pedro Castro reminded as his old correspondent, though later he had gone by the name of Arthur Orton.
