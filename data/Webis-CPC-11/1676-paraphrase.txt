the thing that i am giving you next is an extract from the Court Rolls of the Borough of Hales Owen, of the Custom of Bride Ale. 

"A payne is made that no person or persons that shall brewe any weddyn ale to sell, may not brewe aboue twelve stryke of mault at the most, and that the said persons so married may not keep nor haue above eyght messe of persons at hys dinner within the burrowe, and before hys brydall daye he shall keep no unlawfull games in his house nor out of his house on payne of 20s."
