She has an appealing way with her that can turn even that (at least the more endurable aspects of it) to favor and prettiness. And in The Land Girl's Love Story (HODDER AND STOUGHTON), a theme after her own heart, she has given us what is, I think, her best success so far. It is an outstanding slight tale of two heroines who took their patriotic turn at the work of the land army on a Welsh farm, and the adventures, agricultural and (of course) amorous that befell them there.
