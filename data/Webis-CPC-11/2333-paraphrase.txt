The Khan's trusted horse picked his way over the rocks carefully. However, Ammalát's diligent horse was not up to the task, since he was raised on Daghestán soil so different from the terrain here. 

Without access to his hay and missing his daily grooming, two days of fleeing through the dangerous rocks, biting cold and intense unfiltered sun of the mountains was taking a serious toll on Ammalát's horse.
