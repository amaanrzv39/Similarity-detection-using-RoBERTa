The fire happend on the 9th of june,and as the houses of the town were mostly built of wood, it soon spread beyond the power of any efforts which the population could command to restrain it.The custom-house Romen catholic church protestant episcopal cathedral,court-house,jail ordnance store,allthe newspaper and printing-offices,the banks,the hall of legislature, post-office,and police-office,were all burnt clown,together with two whole streets,each more than a mile long, leaving 12,000 persons without a habitation.
