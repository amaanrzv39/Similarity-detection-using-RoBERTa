The entire incident, the scene and the time, was wild, interesting and filled with alacrity, with just a tad of the awful to regard an additioanl avidity. It was a black stormy night, and the wind felt to be screaming abve our heads ripping the trees apart. A red glimmering shine was casted on the rocks by the torches in our very neighbourhood, which gave the depths of the gloomy forest an haunting indescribable feel. The far halloos of men who were invested in search of their comrades, stood wildly upon the breeze, and occasionally fired shots banged the rocky wild with an everlasting echo.
