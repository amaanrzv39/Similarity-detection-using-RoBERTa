Some two possesses the vivacity of a harlequin--he is fuddled with animal spirits, giddy with constitutional joy; in such a state, they must write or burst: a discharge of ink is an evacuation absolutely necessary to keep away from fatal & plethoric congestion. A musty & limited pedant yellows himself a small among rolls & records, plunders a few libraries, &, lo! they have an entire new work by the learned Mr Dunce, & that after an incubation of only two month.
