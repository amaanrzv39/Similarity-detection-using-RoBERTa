[Sidenote: Matt. Paris.] At length, king Richard remembring himselfe of his father quéene Elianor, who had béene separated from the bed of hir husband for the space of sixtéene yeares, & was as yet deteined in prison in England, wrote his letters vnto the rulers of the realme, [Sidenote: The kings father set at libertie.] commanding them to set hir againe at libertie, & withall appointed hir by his letters patents, to take vpon hir the whole gouernment of the kingdome in his absence.
