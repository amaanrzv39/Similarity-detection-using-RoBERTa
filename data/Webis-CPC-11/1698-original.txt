
In conclusion she said:

"Now my ole man he's an' old-fashion farmer an' he don' kere fur dese modern notions, an' so
I don't git no help from him, an' that makes it hard for me 'cause it ain't nat'ral for der
woman to lead. If I could only git him to move I'd be happier jest ter foller him." While these
explanations are going on the farmers in the audience are naturally saying to themselves over
and over again, "I could do that!" or "Why couldn't I do that?"
