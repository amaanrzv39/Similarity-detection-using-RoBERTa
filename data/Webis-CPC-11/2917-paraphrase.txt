In his own style, he told Colonel M'Mahon, the Prince's secretary--"I made him, and I shall unmake him."

The prince was walking together with Lord Moira in St James's Street. Before them was walking arm to arm Brummel and also a man of rank. The prince didn't want to be recognised. But Brummell asked about him by saying "Pray, who is your fat friend?" This "fat friend" hit was very pungent. It was more pungent in reality than in its usual from.
