It did, & now that her boy has grown to be a man they comes to her in his officer's uniform to say,... Ah, these partings! They are the death-hours of their dear ones, & the women know it, although, like Andromache, they go on "smiling through their tears."
With what brave & silent hearts they face the sequel ! The sister of Sub-Lieutenant So-and-So receives letters from him every other week. Such cheerful small pencil scribblings! "Dearest Sister, I have a jolly comfortable dug-out now--three planks & a truss of straw, & I sleep on it like a top." Or, perhaps, "You see they have sent me back to the Base after three weeks under fire, & now I have a real, real room, & a real, real bed!" The dear elderly darling!
