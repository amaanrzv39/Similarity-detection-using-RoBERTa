But now the message of the prophet had in it testimony as well as warning:

"Have we not warned you for three years and a half, that the man, Apleon, whom you have all
trusted in, was but the tool of his father, the Devil?  Have we not told you often that he
worked upon your deluded minds and imaginations for one purpose only, to keep you from 'The
God of Salvation,' and that, presently, he would set up his own image to be worshipped in that
gilded thing of unbelief, upon that mount, yonder?"
