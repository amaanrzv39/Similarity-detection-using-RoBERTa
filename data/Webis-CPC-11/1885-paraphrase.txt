"Ah! some hopes there, Money--What is that you hold?" "A Poularde, Sir." "Obliged, Crowns--no Pull-hard thank you, devilish hard I doubt--Mem: fowl called Pull-hard at Gin--Try again, my man." "A Dindon and dans son jus, Sir." "Ding dong and a dancing Jew!--sort of stewed Rothschild, I suppose--Well! if I don't mean exactly to starve, I fear I must even venture on the Jew.--Not bad, by Long--Mem: Dancing Jews in sauce capital--mention that to young G----, of the Tenth."
