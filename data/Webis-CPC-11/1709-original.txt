As she
came broadside to the wind in turning, it seemed as if she must be blown over, so violently
did she careen. Once safely round, she flew before the wind, which now became her ally instead
of her enemy, and by its aid she was soon abreast of Borja Bay. Never was there a more sudden
transition from chaos to peace than that which ensued as she turned in from the tumult in the
main channel to the quiet waters of the bay. The Hassler almost filled the tiny harbor shut
in between mountains.
