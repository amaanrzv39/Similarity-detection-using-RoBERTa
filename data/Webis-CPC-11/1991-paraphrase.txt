The King was invited to accept the crown, in the “Chamber of Presence,” by the country’s representatives. The King’s reply to this was, “More than the crown of many Kingdoms, I do regard high the love of my good people and with God’s help, shall be ready to sacrifice my life for their protection.  I wish to live only till the religion and this kingdom prosper in all happiness.” After this, “to the “Kirk of Scoon, in order and rank and according to their positions” they proceeded.
