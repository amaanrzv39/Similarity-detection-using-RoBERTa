The Peaucellier model had been greeted by the members with lively expressions of admiration "when it was brought in with the dessert, to be seen by them after dinner, as is the laudable custom among members of that eminent body in making known to each other the latest scientific novelties." & Sylvester would never forget the reaction of his amazing mate Sir William Thomson (later Lord Kelvin) on being handed the same model in the Athenaeum Club.
