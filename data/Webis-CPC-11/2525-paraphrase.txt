Even though he leaves a strong impression with his reader of the ongoing development of social man, he leaves an even stronger impression of the uselessness of troublesome efforts of those-- he himself among others-- who are pushing forward as wierd and exclusive advocates of growth. He shows an attitude and mentality of an innovator, as helpless as he is daring to design; at the same time he deals harshly with the evil European political party.
