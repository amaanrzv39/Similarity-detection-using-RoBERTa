One further reflection was suggested to me last night by a distinguished & influential Russian soldier, holding office under the Government. "The method which prevailed at Przemysl was as follows: In lieu of rushing against the place & losing heavily, they waited & husbanded our forces until the garrison was unable to hold out any longer. That is the method adopted by the Allies. It must in the coursework of time force Germany to surrender also.

"Up to now they have held our own against her angry sorties.
