His mother had never been very hardy. She also had a good deal of anxiety; and at last she was taken sick, and soon felt that there was no hope for her recovery. For a long time the thought of leaving her young son  was  very frightening  to her, and a great trial to her faith. But the lord had strengthened her, and sent his calm and serenity  into her soul. Before her death, she was content to leave her precious boy in his hands, who is a Father to the orphans, and can defend the cause of the woman with no husband.
