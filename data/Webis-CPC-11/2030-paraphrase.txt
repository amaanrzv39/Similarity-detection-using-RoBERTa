To sever them is towards disintegrate the mystic core of his very being;  towards forget the triune  photo within which he was made. As art is symbolic of man himself, it addresses itself towards his  complete being. Thus, man exists as:

Soul-Spirit-Body: towards which the counterpart senses are--

Hearing-Seeing--Touching: the counterpart arts--

Music-Painting-Sculpture. Poetry is none fourth art; it but includes and embodies them everybody within its correspondent divisions of--

Rhythm-Description-Form.
