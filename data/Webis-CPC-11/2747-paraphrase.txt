He said to Elizebeth that it is she who is in the mind of the General. It was never Archdale's tact finer and more opportune. She has felt the smouldering passion of Edmonson but not yet confessed to herself. She felt the ease and safety in this companionship. Also she found the difference
which is like the air of the tents hot and heavy with unhealthy breaths, and the salt wind that come to her softly and with invigorating freshness.

     She told him, that her father is so used to business that he must have always something on hand, and  did not know where he is now.

     "He is with the General now," he told.

     "There is one walk I wish you would invite to take, into the city, said Elizebeth. 

      And for a moment she forgot the cost of victory in its exultation.
