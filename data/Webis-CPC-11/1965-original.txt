We Asiatics, have a proverb, probably handed down to us, and by the Hindus repeated ignorantly
as to its esoteric meaning.  It has been known ever since the old Rishis mingled familiarly
with the simple and noble people they taught and led on.  The Devas had whispered into every
man's ear--Thou only--if thou wilt--art "immortal."
