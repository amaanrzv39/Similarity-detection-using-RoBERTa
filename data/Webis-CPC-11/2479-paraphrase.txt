This is for your attention.How much better, if they had not been acting against their health all the year round.It is extremely difficult to teach our people to eat healthily. You will find no difficulty to persuade them to take medicine.

People have always time to swallow a pill, but you will certainly have trouble to teach them to chew with leisure. How many people who find time every year to spend the season at Vichy will tell you it is quite impossible for them to spend five minutes more every day at luncheon time. 

And nevertheless they would regain these few minutes a day with interest, if they would avoid that host of maladies which will stop them one day in the midst of their occupations.
