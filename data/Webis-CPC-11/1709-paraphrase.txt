As she came broadside to the wind in turning, it seemed as if she must be blown over, so aggressively did she careen. Once safely round, she flew before the wind, which now became her ally in its place of her enemy, and by its aid she was soon abreast of Borja Bay. Never was there a more unexpected transition from chaos to peace than that which ensued as she turned in from the tumult in the main channel to the calm waters of the bay. The Hassler almost filled the tiny harbor shut in between mountains.
