In answerng his daughters question of wether or not he was asleep, the prince replied, "Oh no!Only I have such sweet thoughts." He was often to be found with his hands folded in the position of prayer and the air of serenity that surrounded him made it clear to all present that those 'sweet thoughts' remained with him til the end.

It became clear sometime in the afternoon of the 14th december, that the end was imminent. his last words were "Good little wife," spoken tenderly to the Queen. Shortly afterwards she answered "It is little wife." By this point, unable to speak the prince merely bowed his head in response.
