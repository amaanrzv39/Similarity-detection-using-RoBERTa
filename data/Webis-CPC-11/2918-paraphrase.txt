The "fat friend" hit was more pungent in reality than in its usual form. The prince determined as he was walking down St. James street with Lord Moira and seeing Brummell walking with a man of rank, to show the oppeness of the conflict. He stopped and talked to the noble lord, appearing to have never noticed the Beau before. At the instant he began turning away, Brummell asked in his most clear voice, "Pry tell, who is that fat friend of yours?" Nothing could be any less tactful; for it paid back the Princes apparent need for fame exactly in his own game, and also gave him a sting in his sensitive spot.
