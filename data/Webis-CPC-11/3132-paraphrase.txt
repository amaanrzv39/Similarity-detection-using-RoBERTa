"You must not turn your head, regardless of what you hear. You could lose your life and eternal salvation if you do. Is that clear?"

"I do."

The darkness and emptiness surrounded them like a thick curtain. Under their feet the marsh heaved with groanings that sounded like a wierd and evil beast. The waters beneath them swirled menacingly.

Job muttered "These are the very gates of hell!"

As soon as he spoke they heard the chilling howl of the dog that made Job's blood run cold. The dog strained on its leash with an otherworldly power trying with its might to bite Job.
