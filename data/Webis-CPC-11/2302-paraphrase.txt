He started off again, without thinking where to look for Hetty.  Rushing around desperately calling out her name and calling for Cham.

The Ledge was an area he stopped at and look down the high part.  Hark! He heard the noise of a dog.  He walked down a little and shouted.  The dog's whine called out at him again.  With a fresh look he called and the dog made a noise exmely loud and recognized it to be Cham.
