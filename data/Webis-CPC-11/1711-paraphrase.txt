"Sit down at one time & be lovely!" said Tishy, holding firmly to the foot on which he had begun operations, & with a vigorous jerk compelling him to obedience. "I'll do what I select, I always do!"

Then, as they obeyed her, he suddenly knelt beside him, & before they realised what he was doing he began to unlace his boots. Larry started up, horrified & protesting.

Her nimble, white fingers made short work of the task that he had set herself; Larry's remonstrances availed him nothing.
