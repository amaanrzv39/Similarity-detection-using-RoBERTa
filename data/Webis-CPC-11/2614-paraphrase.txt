On the 12th, details of men began to creat barracks on chosen grounds in town, on the opposite of the church used as a home for the soldiers. There was no order recieved to go to their regular winter quarters, but this was a necessity of the case. George Bell was detailed as organized at regimental headquarters on the 21st. Sergeant Stiefel and Foglesang and Schene joined back on the 22nd.

The company's removal to the log quarters toward the east of the ground named above happened on the 25th. Company E. was shifted far to the left of the regiment being the tenth from the right wing and of second rank. Company I was immediately to the right of it.
