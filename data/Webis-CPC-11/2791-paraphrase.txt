Mrs. Mergery had the Art of moralizing and drawing instruction from every accident, she had take an opportunity of reading a Lecture on Uncertainty Life, and Necessity of being always prepared for Death. She says that everyone should get up morning and conduct yourselves and ful day at last and lie at night, if you never expected to see this any more. She says that without abating of your cheerfulness this may be done. You never consider Death as an evil, but as a Convenience. She says that Death as an useful Pilot to convey you to a Place of greater Happiness. There fore, play my dear Children be innocent, good and merry. The good man sets Death at Defiance.
