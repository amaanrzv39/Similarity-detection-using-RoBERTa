He had thrown up the sponge altogether when the war broke put and "gone yellow", was living from hand to mouth among the chinese. A ship at the end of August touched at the Far Eastern Port, picking up volunteers for the Western Front. Though the port contributed a goodly number, there remained one berth vacant. The long suffering consul had a stroke of inspiration. There was a means of swelling at once the man power of his country and riding himself of a pestilent ne'er-do-well. His boys searching far and wide, discove5ed John Fanshawe in the back premises of a Malay go-down, oblivious to all things, and bore him inanimate abroad ship.
Our hero answer The Call in this manner.

He appeared, in due course, in our reverse squadron and was detailed to my troop.
