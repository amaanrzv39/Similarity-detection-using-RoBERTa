The two amusing imps giggled and shook their heads with genuine enjoyment that this world could not give! Johnny slid off his shoes and stockings and quickly put them on the infant Florry. He secured them from falling off with a big cord. This added to their satisfaction. 
"We can play cubby house in the stone mound," softly spoke Florry. 
"Hol' on, till I carry in this wood," said Johnny. "You better hide till I get back." 
Johnny promptly delivered his load with an eagerness he had never shown before.
