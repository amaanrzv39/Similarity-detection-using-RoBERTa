"But, you were going to stay for four weeks," said his host.

"Yes--but I have to leave--I believe I'm in love with your countess."

"Well, my friend, I can't help that. I was in love with her also two decades ago," replied the agreeable husband. "But is she in love with you?"


The admirer lowered his eyes, and, in humble boldness, said softly, "I believe so."

"I see! That changes things. I shall send for your post-horses. Good day."
