"Ah, mother, darling, we are alone now; you
will stay with me till I die.  I am tired."

"I feared that their visit would excite you too much, my child."

"O no, mother; I couldn't bear to die without seeing them, I loved them so much.  Mother, will
you sing to me a little--sing me my favourite hymn."

She stopped, for sobs choked her voice.  "I am sorry I cannot, Johnny. But I cannot bear to
think how soon we must part."
