So, too, it may be well believed, would it be with China, Japan, Bokhara, & the Sandwich Islands. Of what use, however, would be such an union? Would it increase the facilities for transplanting the ideas of American authors? Are not the obstacles to such transplantation already sufficiently great, & is it desirable that they ought to be at all increased? Germany has already tried the experiment, but whether or not, when the time shall come, the existing treaties will be renewed, is doubtful. Where they now pays dollars, they probably receives cents. Discussion of the query there has led to the translation & republication of the letters here now republished, & the views therein expressed have received the public approbation of men whose opinions are entitled to the highest consideration.
