In our store we have all varieties of fish, meat, fowl, fruit, dulces, and wines.

A good way to fill up long periods of time is to read, write, sit or walk upon the deck, whatever suits the individual, or by drinking orange juice, or by taking long naps, or by any other ingenious or random way to kill time.  At five we had a dinner where no one joins us but the captain and one officer; and after dinner we all go to the deck to until bed-time, where we will walk around, or look out to the sky and sea, or listen to the long sad songs of the sailors.
