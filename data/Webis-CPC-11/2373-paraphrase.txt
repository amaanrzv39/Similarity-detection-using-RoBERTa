Punch to the Prophet.

[Mr. RAVENSTEIN, at the British Association, thought about the query, how long it will be before the world becomes over-populated.]

Prophet of o'er-population, your ingenious calculation, Causeth discombobulation only in the anxious mind That forecasts exhausted fuel, or the period when the duel Will have given their final gruel to Italian journalists; a kind Of cantankerous, rancorous spitfires, blusterous, braggart, boyish, blind, Who much mourning scarce would find.
