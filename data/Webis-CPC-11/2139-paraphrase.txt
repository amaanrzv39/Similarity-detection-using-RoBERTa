Thus, a sort of oral encyclopedia is ordered, an universal exposition of human knowledge, a permanent exposition constantly improved and open, to which its visitors, provided with a certificate of average instruction as an entrance ticket, will see with their own eyes, besides recognized science that which is under of formation, besides discoveries and proofs the way of discovering and proving, namely the method, history and broad progress, the place of each science in its group, and of this group its place in the general whole.
