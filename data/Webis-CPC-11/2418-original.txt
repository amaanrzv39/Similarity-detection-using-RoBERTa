Not the least of the reasons militating against complete success was the short time possible
for psychoanalytic treatment. The patient was seen only three weeks.  As the time needed for
a psychoanalysis is variable depending on the particular patient, it is clear that this would
be too short a time to enable a young girl, only recently here from Russia, to understand,
or to overcome resistances.
