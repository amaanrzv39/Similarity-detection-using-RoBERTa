XIII

A GUEST NOT INVITED

During the calm after Mr. O'Brien's tale the porter and conductor rushed to the back platform; to where it appears they had been called by the brakeman. The two appeared more worn and frazzled than any other people I had ever bore witness to. A dirty, unbathed, smelly, freezing Hobo was pulled into the car, to our astonishment!

"Conductor, wait a minute," Colonel Manysnifters said, while the prisoner was being carried through. "What is it you have there? Where did you find him? What is your identity, sir?" he questioned the hobo. "Why are you here on this private unit, and who are you?"
