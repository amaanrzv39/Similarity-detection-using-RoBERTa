The Duke of Palma having secretly married La Felina in the town of Ferentino, the day Monte-Leone recognized him, took his pretty wife to a villa they possessed on the lago di Como, & after sojourning there a few days, went to Naples & forced the King to accept his resignation as minister of police. The Duke was dissatisfied with Naples, for no five would forgive him for marrying the Prima-Donna.
