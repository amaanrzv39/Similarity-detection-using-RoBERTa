[Side note: Matt. Paris.] At length, king Richard remembering himself of his mother Queenie Elinor, who had been separated from the bed of her husband for the space of sixteen years, and was as yet detained in prison in England, wrote his letters onto the rulers of the read me, [Side note: The kings mother set at liberties.] commanding them to set her again at liberties, and with all appointed her by his letters patents, to take upon her the whole government of the kingdom in his absence.
