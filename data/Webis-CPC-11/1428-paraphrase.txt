Well, keep in mind I'm ready to lend you a hand, and pull you through when things get desperate." The smile with which this offer was received had no result upon my companion. He took it rather as a tribute to the subtle humor which, as he believed, lay lurking in his simplest utterances. "Always make 'me laugh," he observed, with pride. "It keeps up the spirits of these poor devils of rowing-men; and old BULLEN knows I'm all there when I'm wanted."
