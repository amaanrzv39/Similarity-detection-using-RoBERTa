'I have been wondering ever
since I came here at the splendid faces and figures of men, women, and children, which popped
out upon me from every door in that human rabbit-burrow above.  I have been in raptures at
the gracefulness, the courtesy, the intelligence of almost everyone I meet; and now, to crown
all, everyone among them seems to be a musician.'

'Really you are not far wrong, and you will find them as remarkable morally as they are physically
and intellectually.
