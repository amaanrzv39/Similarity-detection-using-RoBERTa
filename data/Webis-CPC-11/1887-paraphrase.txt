Money came in slowly, but ran out so fast that in November Congress structured weekly returns from the Treasury, not, of sums on hand, but of what parts of the last emission remain unexpended. The campaign of '77 was at hand; how the campaign of '76 would close was yet unsure. The same impenetrable veil that hid Trenton and Princeton from their eyes concealed the disaster of Fort Washington and the Jerseys. They still looked with anticipation to the lower line of the Hudson.
