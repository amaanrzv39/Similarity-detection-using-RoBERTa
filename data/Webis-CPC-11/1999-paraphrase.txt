Added to these was another possessed of the prestige and power that wealth confers--very conservative, timid, cautious, self-satisfied, and dreading innovations of popular rule, but republicanism. Amid these five classes, and sprinkled among the rank and file, was found a sentiment very patriotic, with those who saw nothing worth living for outside of the purview of the "tight small island."
There seems a fate in the propriety of territory changing dominion.
