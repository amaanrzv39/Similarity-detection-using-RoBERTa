He disappeared in the great whirl of men and other circumstances
which fills this wonderful country. From time to time, during five years, I had made inquiries
concerning him of mineralogists, botanists, and other vagrant characters, without getting the
smallest hint as to his whereabouts. At last he had turned up as the private prophet of three
middle-aged widows.

"Jenny," said I to my wife, "do you remember the night I frightened you so and kissed you as
you lay in a fainting-fit?"
