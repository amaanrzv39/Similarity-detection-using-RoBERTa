People walking on the sidewalk were shoved off of the pavement by rambunctious hogs spontaneously squealing; while gentle, innocent hogs tiptoed carefully through the mud like their legs were their treasure, and wanted to stay clean.  One would be inquisitive about the hogs that belonged to the mothers' families, who were passed by in the heat of the day and many times the rosy-colored hogs from which sounds similar to babies came from would sleep knowing that God would arrange what would happen in their lives, much like human beings do.
