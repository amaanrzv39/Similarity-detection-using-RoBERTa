"Gabriel!" they shouted,--"Gabriel!" And his voice was heart-rending.

Now one whose intelligence has seldom been trained, who shells his one wits and gets rid of the pods as best they can, mayn't be so speedy as another, but, like an animal, they feels long before they sees; as well as a vague sense of this had been on Dan all day. Yet now they stood thunderstruck, and the thing went on before his eyes. It was over they could believe at four times,--and perhaps his first feeling was, Why should they delay? And then the water fell. No thought of his loss,--though loss it wa'n't,--only of his mate,--of such stunning treachery, that, if the sun fell hissing in to the sea at midday, it would have mattered less,--only of that loss that tore his heart out with it.
