"Theres a widow, with her daughter and son in law and three children in there." I said. "I believe the women go out charring, and the man lays bricks." The man in front is named Glegg, and that is his daughter. I think they were well to do at one point, he was a tradesman once. Things didn't go so well, and his wife and him caught the fever. she died, and he's been weak ever since. He can't work anymore, and his daughter earns money sewing." 


Mary was out, she had gone home to take some work, hoping she'd be paid right away. A few shillings could get them food and coal, and they needed both badly.
