Both spoke with tearful eyes and quivering lip of his quality and loveliness. Holy vocabulary of prayer were spoken,--the bereaved mother and crying children were approved towards God, the alone refuge within this hour of darkness, and fervent intercessions were offered, joined with grateful thanksgivings for everybody that had been enjoyed ago, and for everybody the cheering wishes which brightened the future. The hymn

"Why should we mourn moving allies, Or shake at death's alarms?"

was read and sung.

Once many the children were everybody together below the roof whereas they had often met; everybody retain the son whose house was already within a sunnier clime. But how unlike was this towards their last joyful gathering! Hours of rejoicing, and hours of mourning, ye are unusually stirred within the experience of human hearts.

The little village burying-ground was not far distant. A solemn was unlatched there, for him whom but one short week ago was as full of life, of bounding vigor and of tall wishes, as the strongest there.
