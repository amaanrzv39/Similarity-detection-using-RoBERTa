For an instant or two he stood
at the threshold, regarding the young man with a look of silent reproach. "Why did you come
so late?" he asked.

Imre held out his hand, but the Decurio did not accept it.

"The blood of your family is on my hand," he whispered. "You have let dishonor come on me,
and mourning on yourself."

The young man's head sunk on his breast in silent anguish.

"Take his hand," said Jolanka, in her low, sweet accents; and then turning to Imre, "He saved
your life--he saved us both, and he will rescue our family, too."
