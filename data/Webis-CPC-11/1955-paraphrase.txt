We were none of us profound sufficient to quote or produce any facts from the monuments and records of the early convert to account for the expression; but I visualize it probable that a solution could be readily given by some of your learned correspondents. The burning of the dead does not emerge to be in itself an anti-Christian ceremony, nor essentially connected with Pagan idolatries, and therefore might have been tolerating in the case of Gentile believers like any other indifferent usage. 
CINIS.
