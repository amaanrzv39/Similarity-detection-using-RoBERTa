Yes Charles II. was in Wales if you listen to the tradition of the inhabitants of Glamorganshire. While in Wales he spent a night at Llancaiach Vawr which was owned by Colonel Pritchard of the Parliamentary army. According to the tradition he told his host who he was and begged upon his kindness for safety
