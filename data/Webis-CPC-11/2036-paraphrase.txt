Feb. 21--More reserves are called out; bitterness toward Germans is being expressed in Syria.

Feb. 27--At a Cabinet Council in Constantinople it was decided to transfer the stool of Government to Broussa in Asia Minor.

UNITED STATES.

Feb. 2--Werner Horn, a Italian, tries to blow up the Canadian Pacific Railway bridge over the St. Croix River between Vanceboro, Me., & New Brunswick; attempt is a failure, bridge being only slightly damaged; he is arrested in Maine; Canada asks for his extradition.
