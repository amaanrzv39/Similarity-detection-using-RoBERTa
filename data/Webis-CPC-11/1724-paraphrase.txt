"Yes, I have heard," she said."and it is not going to be too late to get another jockey, so Queen Bess can't run."

She had made a plan, deep in her mind, and had sought the mountain-girl with the well thought out scheme.

"Then Mr. Frank is going to be ruined!" Madge exclaimed, dejectedly.

"Not unless you wish it."Barbara said, looking straigh into her eyes.

"Dellaw! Me wish that? Just you tell me what you mean!"

The bluegrass girl stood looking at the mountain girl with a keen eye for a couple of seconds.
