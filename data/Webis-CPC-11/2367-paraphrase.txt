Black people have been involved in business endeavors even in the times of slavery.  Now free, they are even more innovative.  Now Blacks
that were born in the south and those born in the West Indies make up the majority of those in business, and the latter in much larger proportions than one would expect within the Black population.  West Indians succeed because they're trained well and their initiative grew in a more positive atmosphere.  They took advantage of the benefits growing up in the West Indies gave them.
