What a mockery of destiny!...Goodbye forever,Luna!...No, not Luna. Goodbye, Horabuena!

The following day he set sail on the boat that was heading for Port Said. What was there for him to do in Gibraltar?...For three months it had been paradise, with a woman that inhanced his life; now it was an unbearable city, stifling and dull; an abandoned fortress; a dark, damp cell. He wired to his uncle, telling him of his leave.
