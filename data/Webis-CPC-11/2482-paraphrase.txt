We rest in San Giovanni there is a chance of getting away from it.  The Serbian government close our heels once they caught us. On That Monday evening we were sitting with Frenchmen. They were depressed both of them, at last Tweedledum said that They shall never reach Paris, they shall be there for ever.
At that time Jan replied rashly that he think they ought to be home in a week.
Dum put on the superior French air. Dum said that "Vous croyez?" Jan said that he will be bet on it. Dum answered that a dinner, Jan replied "Good".
