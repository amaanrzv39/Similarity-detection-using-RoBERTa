This is a letter of king Richard directed to the States of the land for the deposing of the bishop of Elie from his office of lord chancellour. 
Paris. In time he was deposed from his office of being chancellour, and not without warrant, for in verie deed, king Richard hauing receiued aduertisements from the lords and peeres of the realme, of the chancellours presumpuous and hautie demeanour, with wrongs offered to diuerse persons, wrote to them againe as followeth.
