It might be hoped that Carmela's mood would soften when she discovered her rival's hapless
love, but that would be expecting something which her bursting southern heart could not give.
A volcano pours forth lava, not water.  It scorches, not heals.  Iris, willing or not, had
sapped her Salvador's allegiance.
