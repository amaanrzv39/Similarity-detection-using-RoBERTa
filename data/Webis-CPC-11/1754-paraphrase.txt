He put up a sawmill and had all the timber ready to complete a grist mill, when they was cut short in his early life, and his wife was cast on the mercy of Providence. They lived six miles from us. Lots of of those whom I knew were mill hands. Gilchrist who was killed, was an employee of Mr. Gowanlock.
Frog Lake is huge. I know that in three direction it is twelve miles long. In the centre of the lake is a huge island, that is clothed in a garb of evergreen.
