After a short silence, Adelaide asked again, ‘He was certainly unaware of its contents?’
Madame d’Heranville replied, ‘He seemed to be, and I shall inform the police office tomorrow. Perhaps our proof may be helpful to him.’
‘Tomorrow!’ Adelaide keeping the thoughts to herself. ‘And tonight he must bear all the agonies of anticipation!’
Adelaide wondered if her face was pale and faded like Madame d’Heranville facial expression shows, should her heart also be like hers.
