N  ETYMOLOGICAL DICTIONARY OF SCRIPTURE NAMES   is purely based on tone of voices and it is explained with Copious Illustrative Notes and Introductory Observations on the Origin of Language and Proper Names.  This is done by W.G. Hird.  This has been highly recommended by some authors and companies such as Rev. Drs. Eadie, Forbes, Hannah, Hill, Acworth and Godwin, Revs. Daniel Walton, W. Bird, J. D. Brocklehurst, T. Rowland, Jonathan Glyde, J. G. Miall and W. Cooke and company. Besides it has been recommending by some other authors like London: Hamilton, Adams and Co., Simpkin and Marshall, Arshall, Edinburgh, Oliver and Boyd and Glasgow.G. Gallie.
