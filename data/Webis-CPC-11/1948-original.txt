
'Only,' I continued, playing my part with as much skill as I possessed, and more than I could
have believed myself capable of a few days ago, 'I don't want to get locked up.'

'No, no,' he answered, 'I don't want you to get locked up either, Jacky. I should miss you,
you know, very much. But you act sensibly, and you will be all right. What's more, I will show
you how to make your fortune before we have done.'

'I should like to make a fortune,' I said, with perfect truth.
