Finally the bell rang and Mrs. Lockton walked upstairs and leading with a gentleman, who is not that much good looking.

When Mrs. Lockton and that gentleman reached Louise Mrs. Lockton said, "i am sorry to be so late" and let me introduce Mr. ---to you. And whether she had forgotten the name, Mrs. Bergmann did not care at the time but it was impossible catch it. Mrs. Bergmann absent mindedly shook hands with him and looking at the clock the time was ten minutes to two.

I have been deceived, she thought to herself and anger rose in her breast like a wave.
