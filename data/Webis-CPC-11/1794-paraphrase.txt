Men overdo etiquette sometimes, because of a conventional fear of slipping up somewhere. There was a nice Red Cross major in Germany who had had no instruction in military matters, and had no arrogance whatever. So he used to salute all the privates and the M. P.'s before they had a chance. He was usually asking the road to somewhere or other, and they would stand staring after him thoughtfully until he was out of sight.
