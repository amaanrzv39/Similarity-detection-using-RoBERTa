Hozier suggested into the girl's ear, "The men knew". He dared not trust either his temper or his vocabulary. 

Hurrying on, Iris said "We shall lose no time, now, monsieur."

"This way then. No, we do not pass the cave. We go right round the cliff. Permit me, mademoiselle. I am acquainted with each step."

He took her hand and Phillip followed. He was young enough to long for an opportunity to tell San Benavides that he was yet a puppy, a mongrel puppy.
