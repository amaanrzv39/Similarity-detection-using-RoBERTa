It happened in such a case as this that there was a doubt in the minds of the bystanders about the shoulder she intended to be taken by one of the friends.	They looked at her; but she lost her voice irretrievably and they felt that as far as this point was concerned the management had allowed them full discretion. But it was not so: the dying woman was not able to speak: but she moved one of her hands convulsively and touched the left shoulder and died.
