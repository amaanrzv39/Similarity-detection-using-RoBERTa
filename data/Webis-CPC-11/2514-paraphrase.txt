Now, by this time I was intrigued again.  I could now manage to let a few words escape, besides it seemed rude not to reciprocate so much kindness.

"I'm afraid I have been a nuisance."  I said.  I can only express how truly grateful I am"

His round face flushed a little, and his motions were a tiny bit unsteady.

"I am not sure how I could have done anything differently than I...we have already done."  he replied, in a soft spoken voice.  "We were all grateful to do what we could.  I won't say it was enjoyed, due to the fact that it was our busiest time of year", but, he said with a chuckle, fearing his expression was misinterpretted, "I don't suppose you have enjoyed this either, seeing how you are so far from your home."
