We are too inclined to believe that our forefather were as rude as their speech, and their speech as they; but this concourse of grammatical delicacies, retained for centuries after the subjection of the native language by seduction, and systematically applied in the versification of the great old poet, shows a feeling of language, and an vertible stamp of art, that claim the most genial and agree respect of a refined breed, to their not wholly unrefined, more heroic ancestors.
