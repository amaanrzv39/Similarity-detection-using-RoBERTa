
"The men knew," suggested Hozier in the girl's ear.  He dared not trust either his temper or
his vocabulary.

"We shall lose no time, now, monsieur," said Iris, hurrying on.

"This way then.  No, we do not pass the cave.  We go right round the cliff.  Permit me, mademoiselle.
I am acquainted with each step."

He took her hand.  Philip followed.  He was young enough to long for an opportunity to tell
San Benavides that he was a puppy, a mongrel puppy.
