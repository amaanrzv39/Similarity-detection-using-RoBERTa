"It is dried wood from the forest. They pile them against the walls and set them in a blaze.
Who is this who tries to check them? By St. Ives! it is the good priest who spake for them
in the hall. He kneels, he prays, he implores! What! villains, would ye raise hands against
those who have befriended you? Ah, the butcher has struck him! He is down!
