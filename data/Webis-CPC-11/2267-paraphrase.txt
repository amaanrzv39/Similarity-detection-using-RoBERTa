My second screw over, they washed. No sooner was that done, than I wanted to see it all over again. "You are very fond of women," they said, "I thought you had seldom had a woman before." Then I explained, gave her the Victoria, & daring said (for they was dressed again), "How I ought to like to do it again." "You take up much time of me, but you may, if you like, at side of de bed." Out came my prick, up it went, her duff & belly in sight now, till I spent in her, & promising to see her again I left.
