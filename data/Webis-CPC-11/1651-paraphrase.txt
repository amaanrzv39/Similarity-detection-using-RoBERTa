Then he let us ride with him, if we wanted, on the animal that was with him, and we flew above walls and churches and such-like, until we came to the grassy field of Blockula.  (That is, Scott's Brockenberg within the Hartz forest.)

We scraped some material from some of the altars and old clocks in a few of the churches we visited.  Afterward he gave us a container of ointment to apply to our skin, and also a saddle along with a wooden nail and hammer to pound the nail into the saddle.
