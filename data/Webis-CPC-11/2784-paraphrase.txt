Mr. Seward sent a despatch to Mr. Adams that they are quite satisfied that their cause was justified given their own resources and ability to maintain this cause. He further added that they did not wish any power to come to their aid in this regard. Their only desire was to sustain the admitted rights and that there should not be any external interference.
