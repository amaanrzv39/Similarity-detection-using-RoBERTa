Now one who has not had his intelligence trained, who hides his wits and disposes the pods to the best of his abilities might not go as fast as the other, but he feels it out long before he sees the same way an animal does, and Dan has been sensing this slightly all day. But he stood still as the event unfolded right in front of him. It was impossible for him to take it all in. Maybe he was thinking, "Why should I hinder?" Then the flood fell. He didn't think about what he lost. He didn't lose anything in fact. Only his friend lost. With such treachery that if the sun fell into the sea during midday, it would have been of no importance to him. The only thing that mattered was the heart breaking loss he experienced.

He shouted in a heart-wrenching voice. "Gabriel! Gabriel!"
