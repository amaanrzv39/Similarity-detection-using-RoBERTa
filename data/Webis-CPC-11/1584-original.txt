
He had not [1]the answer[1] that served him from the Ulstermen, and forasmuch as he had it
not he went on further to the rampart of Emain.

And [2]a second time[2] he had not the response that served him from the men of Ulster. Thus
stood it among the Ulstermen: It was geis for the Ulstermen to speak before their king, geis
for the king to speak before his [3]three[3] druids. Thereafter Sualtaim drove on to the 'Flag-stone
of the hostages' in Emain Macha.
