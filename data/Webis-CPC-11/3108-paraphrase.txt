If maybe the Duke of Coburg and perhaps even the Princess of Prussia were to add a thing, I would keenly yield my total decorative pursuit to these three protectors as a kind of same, and they would have the contentment of having kept me free and geared up for my art. I not able to request for myself neither find the appropriate configuration for the needed accord, but you can, and you and your intercession will succeed. Possible profits from the opera I will draft for Paris I might then wholly commit to the compensation of the obligations I left in Dresden.

Dear Liszt, have I uttered plainly enough?

With the self-confidence of one wholly helpless, I farther request, Make it probable to let me have some wealth before long, in order that I may move out here, depart to Zurich, and survive there till I accept the wished for salary.
