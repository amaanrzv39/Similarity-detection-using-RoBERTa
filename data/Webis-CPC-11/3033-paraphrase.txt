Sieur Raymond deduced his check of the tapestry, and turned with a premonitory cough. "Thus comes to an end the comedy," said he, shrugging, "with much fine, inoffensive chatting about 'always,' while the world triumphs. Invariably the world triumphs, my children. Eheu, we are as God made us, we men and women that cumber His stately earth!" He drew his arm through Raoul's. "Farewell, niece," said Sieur Raymond, smiling; "I rejoice that you are remedied of your malady. Now in regard to gerfalcons--" said he. The arras plunged behind them.

3. Obdurate Love

Matthiette sat brooding in her room, as the after dark was clothed in on. She was pitifully horrified, numb. There was in the room, she dimly found, a weighty peace that sobs had no power to shatter.
