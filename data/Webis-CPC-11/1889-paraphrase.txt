"The master had a town mansion what's pictured in a log books.It was called 'Montebella.' The big columns still distance at The end of Shields Lane.It burnt'about thirty years ago (1937). 

"It's part Injured.I paint got no Nigger nose as' my hair is so long I has to keep it wropped[FN: wrapped]. It's often heard my mummy was redish-looking' with long, straight, black hair. Her paa was a full blooded Choctaw an' mighty nigh as young as she was.I'se been tol'dat nobody dast[FN: dared] meddle with her.
