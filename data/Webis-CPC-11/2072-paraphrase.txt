Dr.Gustavus Brown migrated to maryland in 1708 and in 1710 who was the grandfather of Dr.Brown's married Fraces, the daughter of Colonel Gerard Fawke. Their son,Richard Brown, returned to England to prepare himself for the Church. William, who is Richard's son, was born in Scotland in 1748 and was educated at the University of Edinburgh, got graduated in 1770 and went to America. This is Alexandria's Dr.Brown.

At the age of twenty-two, this young Scotsman, born gentle, learned, traveled, handsome, came to Vigirina.
