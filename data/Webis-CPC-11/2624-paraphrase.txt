CHAPTER XXIII

CREATING CLASS SPIRIT

OUTLINE--CHAPTER XXIII

There are many factors affecting a good spirit inside a class- must be the ambience and appearance of the classroom itself, the teachers' confidence and pupils attitude toward one another, the encouragement of building leaders and good followers and even extra curicular activities involving the whole class. 

Each factors can be as good to many and give positive attitude to the class. These factors affect the pupil in attending their everyday class not only because they are force to go to school. Positive things make a lot of encouragement for a pupil to attend his class and gives him an enthusiasm to participate in most of the class recitals. There are other factors affecting a class spirit, but with the list enlisted above- the class is best encourage and helped by all of in the list.
