However,there is no lack of celebration.the churches were crowded and there are banquets and dances going on everywhere on christmas.The small boys amuse themselves by setting off fireworks in the cities.Dances are frequent during the Christmas week.In the country it will continue for days to the lugubrious  accompaniment of accordions and large drums.the twenty-eighth december that is the Holy innocents day,is all Fools day,which is instead of April first,it being argued that just as the innocents of Herod's day were made to suffer.so innocents were prosecuted of this age.many families receive their friends On New  Year's Eve .There is generally some large ball, and with fireworks and other noises the new year is ushered.

     
The sixth of January is the great day for the children,is also the feast of Epiphany, or Three Kings' Day.It is called SANTO DOMINGO. they now make the rounds and leave presents for deserving children in the memory of the three wise men from the East brought presents to the infant Christ in ages past.this takes place by santa clause.The receptacles they choose for the good things they deliver are either the children's slippers or shoes.letters are written to the Kings explaining what gifts would be acceptable for weeks before the anxiously awaited day, , explaining what gifts would be acceptable, and are given to the parents who undertake to deliver them.
