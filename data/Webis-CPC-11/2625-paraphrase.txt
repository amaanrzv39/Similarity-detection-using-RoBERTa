You probably wouldn't be surprised, if I lengthened my chain a link or two. Out of this age of relaxed discipline sprung forth indulgence to my own notions.Sometimes I might by accident and without unpardonable crime trust as much to my very own, to be very careful and laborious. Although I may be purblinded as to their soaring,and intuitive to their eagle-eyed authority. Modern liberty is precious so it must not be struck by profanity and vularity. Only a choosen few are born to the hereditary and represent the whole democracy, they leave nothing at all to us poor outcasts of the Plebeian race.
