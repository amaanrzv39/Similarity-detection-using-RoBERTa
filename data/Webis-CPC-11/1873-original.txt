"Why, we must get her!" she cried, straightening up. "Why can't we get her?"

"Why," said I, gently as I could--"why, they are--cooking her."

Bessie's cheeks flamed. In less time than it takes to tell it she sprang from the carriage,
burst open the kitchen door, ran against a toddling boy, blindly knocked him over, and faced
Mrs. Beck.

"How did you dare do such a thing!" she almost screamed, seizing the astonished woman by her
dress skirt.
