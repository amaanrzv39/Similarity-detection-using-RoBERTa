I dressed as I did in Amazone seemed to have delighted them, and when  I arrived at the nether garments shop of my horse riding-habit, they all bursted into laughter. Anyway I put a heavy face and ventured every corner of the village where I found the rest of our  party. Our horses were being alternately led up and down by the soldiers, camels with water in goat's skins, baggage beasts, our camp followers, and our free-lances, were drawn up on one side
