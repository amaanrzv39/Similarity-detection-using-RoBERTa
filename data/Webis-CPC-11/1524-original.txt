
"May I go with you?"

"I would rather you did not come, please."

"Why are you so unkind?" I asked, taking her hand. "Tell me you love me, and let us be happy."

"But I don't love you," she said, trying to withdraw her hand, and the tears coming into her
eyes. "I don't love you, and I want to go home." She turned from me to hide her face, looking
about at the same time for some way of escape.

"But you will love me by to-morrow," I replied soothingly.
