What profit would arise to stakeholders? Would England, in case of forcible conquering, not be under the need of obtaining a heavy control in the rise of her South African posts, and so be corrected in seizing a sizeable nobility upon th outcome, which would materially lessen the lucre? What gain would arise to England by replacing an infertile and valuable battle in South Africa for conditions of serenity and fortune, which alone can generate her business profit? England can only educe gain from battles employed between other people. And as to the catalyst of marketable lordship, England, while possessing that a large stretch already, freely and willingly aloows all comers from other citizenships to share the profit with her by her dogma of free business
