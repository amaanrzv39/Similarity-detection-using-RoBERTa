"She said she did not love you then, Luc Michee," said Parpon, interrupting.  "Luc Michee,
you're a fool as well as a sinner. Speak up, Junie."

"I used to tell him that I didn't love him; I only liked him.  I was honest.  Well, I am honest
still.  I love him now."

A sound of joy broke from Luc's lips, and he stretched out his arms to her, but the Cure; stopped
that.  "Not here," he said.  "Your sins must first be considered.  For penance--" He paused,
looking at the two sad yet happy beings before him.  The deep knowledge of life that was in
him impelled him to continue gently:

"For penance you shall bear the remembrance of each other's sins.  And now to God the Father--"
