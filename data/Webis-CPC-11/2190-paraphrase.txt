Again, confronting a grand   contact  whom wore shoes within the morning, he stagnated  and asked him what he had got upon his feet. 'Oh! shoes are they,' quoth he, with a well bred sneer, 'I idea they were slippers.' He was even ashamed of his own brother, and when the latter arrived towards town, begged him towards keep towards the back streets till his novel   clothing were posted home.
