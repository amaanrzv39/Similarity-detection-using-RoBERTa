It may be profitable to the reader to know more about the little know land of kakeikoku which lies in the vicinity of Timbuctoo ( a well known african resort).

A fellow of the Royal Geographical society, whom travel to exotic locations is the norm, has said that Kakekikoku is rich with bewilderments, perils and primitive mysteries of an unexplored African state.  An exclusive folk, warelike in their ways, the Kakekikuans extend a fiery welcome to any stranger who enters their borders.
