The carvings on these doorways are of the heroes and saints of Old Testament,  it represent the historic scenes of  Redemption of the World, it also represent the Gentiles, the Resurrection of the Dead, the Last Judgment, the Condemnation of the Wicked, the Reception of the Just into the habitations of the blest.The Assumption and Coronation of the Blessed Virgin sums up with the legend of series of Christian dogma perpetuated in stone.
