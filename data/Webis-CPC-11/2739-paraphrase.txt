We couldn't tell by the way he was acting whether she was an old friend or some girl he met a few hours ago.  Shiela didn't want to let him go, though, and held onto his hand a bit longer than was necessary.  With her look, she tried to tell convey how much she cared about him and let him know that she was always going to be a friend to him.  But he was avoiding her face, so he didn't get to see it.  He took off at once, quickly getting lost in the crowd that was the Cincinnati Convention.

She was too enmeshed in her own thoughts to notice how nice Mrs. Lorraine and Mrs. Kavanagh were being to her.  She was confused, because he was her long lost friend.  Physically, he was right beside her, but his mind was in a place that she could not touch, and it broke her heart.
