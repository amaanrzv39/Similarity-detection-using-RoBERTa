The bulk of Whittlesey's book, which is four hundred and eighty-seven pages in total, discusses the history of the thirteen or fourteen years preceding the War of 1812.  Prior to this, he discusses over seventy-six pages the arrival of civilized man in Cuyahoga County and commits another fifty pages to discussing the settlers of the Cuyahoga River area.  The final forty pages of the book discuss the history of the few decades that followed the War of 1812.
